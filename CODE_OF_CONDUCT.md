# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free project community.

Contributors should discuss ideas and code in good faith, give actionable feedback, respect differing experience levels, and protect other people's privacy. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and publishing another person's private information are not acceptable.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact method on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
