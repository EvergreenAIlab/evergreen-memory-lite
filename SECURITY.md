# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Use GitHub's private vulnerability reporting feature for this repository. Provide the smallest synthetic reproduction that explains the problem. Do not open a public issue for an unpatched vulnerability.

Never include private or sensitive data in a report. Do not submit secrets, credentials, access strings, real personal documents, financial records, identity documents, or confidential organization material.

## Privacy guidance

This public repository accepts P0 synthetic examples only. P1 through P4 material must not be uploaded to issues, pull requests, discussions, logs, screenshots, or fixtures. See [docs/privacy_model.md](docs/privacy_model.md) for tier definitions.

The maintainers may delete public reports that expose sensitive material and ask the reporter to replace them with a synthetic reproduction.
